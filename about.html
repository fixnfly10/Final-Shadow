Shadow Landscaping Background

Our fully-insured business has been creating artful landscape designs since 1988.Shadow Landscaping Incorporated is proud to serve the McHenry Co, IL area, as well as the surrounding communities. We offer our superb landscaping services to both residential and commercial clients. Whether your yard is a disaster or the beds outside your office need some looking after, Shadow Landscaping is on the job!
